calm

Part 4: Do not dismiss completed cards too quickly

Right now the card completes and disappears pretty fast.

That may be too forgiving and may encourage “tap without actually doing.”

Change completion behavior

When user taps the CTA:

or until the user scrolls away / refreshes / next render naturally clears it

Do not make it feel sticky or broken.
I just do not want it to vanish so fast that the user mindlessly taps through it.
