Enhance the app with subtle color and shape-based interactivity to make the UI feel more alive and premium.

## Core principle

Use color and shapes to communicate meaning, not decoration.

The UI should feel:

* interactive
* responsive
* modern
* premium
* calm

Not:

* flashy
* gamified
* overly colorful

---

## Color system

Use a simple state-based system:

* Green: positive / strong / ready / push
* Amber: moderate / maintain
* Red: low / recover / off track

Use color sparingly and intentionally.

Do NOT:

* fill entire screens with color
* use bright or neon tones
* overuse multiple colors

---

## Daily State styling

Display the Daily State as a small pill or badge:

Example:
[ ● Strong Day ]

* small colored dot
* subtle background tint
* rounded shape

This should be clean and minimal.

---

## Key Drivers

Add subtle color indicators:

Example:

* Sleep ● (green)
* Recovery ● (green)
* Stress ● (red)

Keep indicators:

* small
* minimal
* consistent

---

## Feeling input interaction

When a user selects a feeling:

* apply a subtle color highlight
* animate selection smoothly
* update the plan in real time

Examples:

* “Great” → slight green emphasis
* “Exhausted” → slight red emphasis

Animations should be:

* fast
* smooth
* minimal

---

## Your Day section

Use soft color accents per category:

* Move → subtle blue
* Fuel → subtle orange
* Recover → subtle green
* Mind → subtle neutral or purple

Colors should be:

* muted
* not dominant
* used only for icons or small accents

---

## Background and layout

Optionally apply very subtle background tinting based on state:

* Strong Day → faint green tint
* Recover → faint warm / neutral tint

Keep it extremely subtle.

---

## Micro-interactions

Add:

* soft button press animations
* slight scaling on tap
* smooth transitions when content updates
* subtle loading states

Avoid:

* large animations
* distracting motion

---

## Shapes

Use:

* rounded pills
* soft cards
* minimal dividers
* small dots for indicators

Avoid:

* sharp edges
* heavy boxes
* cluttered layouts

---

## Final goal

The app should feel:

* responsive
* intelligent
* alive
* calm
* premium

Users should feel:
“The app reacts to me and understands my state.”
