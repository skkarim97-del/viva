Ensure stable, mild, moderate, heavier and review-worthy days look and read differently.

Stable day:

Mild symptoms:

Moderate symptoms:
- practical support
- “Support today”

Heavier symptoms:
- amber accents
- stronger support
- care-team review visible if appropriate

Urgent/red-flag:
- red only for true urgent states
