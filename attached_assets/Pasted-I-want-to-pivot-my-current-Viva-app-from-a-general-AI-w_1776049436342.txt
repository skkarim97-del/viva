Activity Decline

Fueling Breakdown

- appetite = very low for 2 to 3 days
- protein confidence = poor
- hydration = poor

Symptom Load

- side effects = moderate or rough for 2+ days

Consistency Breakdown

- missed check-ins rise
- plan completion falls materially
- movement intent and actual pattern diverge repeatedly

- Recovery Breakdown = +25
- Activity Decline = +20
- Fueling Breakdown = +25
- Symptom Load = +20
- Consistency Breakdown = +10

21-40 = mild
