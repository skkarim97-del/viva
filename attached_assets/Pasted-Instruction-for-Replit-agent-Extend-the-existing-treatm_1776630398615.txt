Instruction for Replit agent

Extend the existing treatment status MVP to capture why and when a patient stopped treatment, in a simple, high-signal way.

Goal

We want to understand:

who stopped treatment
why they stopped
when they stopped (early vs late)

This is for internal retention learning during pilots. Keep it lightweight and fast.

Scope

Add two things:

Stop reason (required when stopped)
Stop timing (derived, not manually entered)

Do not expand beyond this.

Feature 1: Stop reason (simple + structured)

When a patient is marked stopped, require a reason.

Allowed values:
side_effects
cost_or_insurance
lack_of_efficacy
patient_choice_or_motivation
other
Requirements:
reason is required when status = stopped
keep existing optional free-text note (important for nuance)
do not add more categories
do not nest or over-structure
Feature 2: Stop timing (derived, not input)

We want to know if churn happened early or late.

Do NOT add a manual dropdown

Instead, derive timing from:

startedAt (or equivalent treatment start date)
treatmentStatusUpdatedAt (when marked stopped)
Compute:
early → stopped within 30 days
mid → 31–90 days
late → 90+ days

Store this as a derived field in analytics, not necessarily persisted unless easy.

Feature 3: Analytics extension

Extend the existing internal analytics dashboard to include:

1. Stop reasons breakdown
count + % by reason
simple list or bar-style display
2. Stop timing breakdown
early vs mid vs late counts
3. Combined view (if easy)
example:
side effects → mostly early
cost → more mid/late

Keep this simple. No need for complex charts.

Feature 4: UI update (clinician dashboard)

In the Treatment Status card:

When status = stopped:

show reason clearly
show optional note
show “stopped X days after starting treatment” or equivalent

No redesign needed. Just extend current card.

Guardrails
do NOT modify mobile app flows unless trivial
do NOT add new dependencies
do NOT change API contracts unnecessarily
do NOT build a full analytics system
do NOT expand taxonomy beyond defined values
do NOT introduce ML or complex inference

This should be a small extension of the current system, not a new system.

Deliverables

After implementation, tell me:

where stop reason is stored and validated
how stop timing is calculated
what changes were made to analytics endpoint
what UI elements were updated
whether anything affects iOS / EAS / build stability
Product intent (important)

We are trying to answer during pilots:

is churn mostly side-effect driven?
does churn happen early in treatment?
are we targeting the right problem?

Keep implementation focused on that.
