🔥 CORE PRINCIPLE

Every rule should follow:

IF [data condition]
THEN [clear action]
WHY [short reasoning]
PRIORITY [high / medium / low]
🧠 RECOVERY / SLEEP RULES
IF sleep < 6.5 hours
THEN reduce training intensity by 30% and avoid HIIT
WHY insufficient recovery increases injury risk and lowers performance
PRIORITY high
IF sleep < 6 hours AND HRV down > 10% from baseline
THEN full recovery day (walking + stretching only)
WHY clear sign of nervous system fatigue
PRIORITY high
IF sleep consistency off (bedtime variance > 1.5 hours past 3 days)
THEN enforce fixed wind-down time tonight (no screens 30 min before bed)
WHY circadian disruption reduces recovery quality
PRIORITY medium
IF sleep > 7.5 hours AND HRV above baseline
THEN green light for high intensity training
WHY body is recovered and ready to perform
PRIORITY high
❤️ HRV / READINESS RULES
IF HRV down > 15% from 7-day average
THEN avoid heavy training and prioritize recovery protocol (walk + hydration + early sleep)
WHY strong indicator of systemic stress
PRIORITY high
IF HRV trending up 3+ days
THEN increase training load or intensity
WHY positive adaptation signal
PRIORITY medium
IF HRV stable BUT resting heart rate elevated > 5 bpm
THEN keep training moderate, avoid max effort
WHY possible early fatigue or illness signal
PRIORITY high
🏃 ACTIVITY / STRAIN RULES
IF high strain yesterday (top 20% of user baseline)
THEN today = low intensity or active recovery
WHY prevents overtraining and improves long-term performance
PRIORITY high
IF 2+ consecutive days of high strain
THEN enforce recovery day
WHY cumulative fatigue risk
PRIORITY high
IF steps < 5,000 by 3pm
THEN schedule 20–30 min walk before dinner
WHY maintain baseline activity and metabolic health
PRIORITY medium
IF no workouts logged in 3 days
THEN schedule 30–45 min workout today (strength or cardio)
WHY maintain consistency and momentum
PRIORITY high
🧠 STRESS / MIND RULES
IF user reports high stress
THEN prescribe 10–15 min downregulation (breathing, walk, or quiet time)
WHY lowers cortisol and improves focus
PRIORITY high
IF high stress AND poor sleep
THEN remove intense training + prioritize recovery + early sleep
WHY compounding stress signals
PRIORITY high
IF low stress AND high energy
THEN encourage deep work or challenging task block
WHY optimal cognitive performance window
PRIORITY medium
🥗 NUTRITION / FASTING RULES
IF fasting AND low energy reported
THEN recommend breaking fast with balanced meal (protein + carbs)
WHY energy deficit impacting performance
PRIORITY high
IF high strain day planned
THEN increase carb intake post-workout
WHY supports recovery and glycogen replenishment
PRIORITY medium
IF hydration low (user input or proxy)
THEN push 16–24 oz water immediately
WHY hydration directly impacts performance and HRV
PRIORITY high
🔄 TREND-BASED RULES (THIS IS WHERE YOU WIN)
IF sleep declining 3+ days in a row
THEN prioritize sleep tonight over training
WHY trend matters more than single day
PRIORITY high
IF HRV declining trend over 5 days
THEN initiate recovery protocol (2 low-intensity days + sleep focus)
WHY early burnout prevention
PRIORITY high
IF workouts consistent 5+ days
THEN suggest deload or lighter day
WHY avoid plateau and fatigue
PRIORITY medium
🎯 DAILY DECISION ENGINE (MOST IMPORTANT)

At the end, synthesize into ONE clear plan:

IF multiple high-priority recovery signals
THEN
- Training: recovery only
- Activity: 8–10k steps
- Mind: 10 min breathing
- Sleep: lights out before 10:30
IF strong recovery signals
THEN
- Training: high intensity or strength
- Activity: normal movement
- Mind: optional
- Sleep: maintain routine
🧪 PRIORITIZATION LOGIC

When multiple rules trigger:

High priority overrides everything
Recovery > performance
Trends > single day data
