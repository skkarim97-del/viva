I’m not convinced Today’s next steps is sensitive enough to changes in the symptom tracking section.

Please make the intervention logic and visible copy more clearly responsive to live symptom changes.

Current concern:
When I change Energy, Appetite, Nausea, Digestion and Bowel Movement, the card updates somewhat, but it does not feel meaningfully different enough across symptom combinations and severities.

Required behavior:
The Today’s next steps card should visibly change based on:
1. Symptom type
2. Severity
3. Symptom combination
4. Recent trend/history if available
5. Apple Health / activity/sleep data if available

Please implement or strengthen a clear decision matrix.

Examples:

A) All good / stable
Energy=Great or Good
Appetite=Strong or Normal
Nausea=None
Digestion=Fine
Bowel Movement=Yes

Expected:
Either hide the intervention card or show a light maintenance card:
Title: Stay steady today
Copy: Your check-in looks steady. Keep following your plan and check in again if anything changes.
No orange warning styling.
No care-team escalation.

B) Mild nausea only
Nausea=Mild
Appetite normal/strong
Digestion fine
Energy good/great

Expected:
Title: Stay ahead of nausea
Copy: Keep meals smaller today and sip water steadily.
Tone should be light and preventive.

C) Moderate nausea + low appetite
Nausea=Moderate
Appetite=Low
Energy not depleted

Expected:
Title: Settle nausea without skipping nutrition
Copy should mention small bland protein-forward snack + slow hydration.
No aggressive escalation unless repeated/worsening.

D) Severe nausea
Nausea=Severe

Expected:
Title: Settle nausea first
Copy should be stronger and more careful:
Start with small sips of water and a few bites of bland food if tolerated.
Care-team review should be more visible if symptoms feel hard to manage, worsening or persistent.
Use amber/heavier styling.

E) Constipation / no bowel movement
Digestion=Constipated OR Bowel Movement=No

Expected:
Card should include digestion/constipation support in More Support:
short walk if able, steady fluids, fiber-rich food.
If nausea is also present, mention that hydration/nutrition/digestion support are connected.

F) Low energy
Energy=Tired or Depleted

Expected:
Card should include energy support:
lighter day, protein with next meal, rest/recovery.
If Apple Health shows low sleep/activity, mention that.

G) Bloating
Digestion=Bloated

Expected:
Do not treat it like constipation.
Use lighter digestion support:
gentle movement, smaller meals, avoid overeating.
Do not say fiber/constipation unless constipation or no bowel movement is selected.

H) Appetite changes
Appetite=Low or Very Low

Expected:
Add appetite support.
If nausea is present, recommend small, bland, easy-to-tolerate foods.
If nausea is absent, recommend small protein-rich meal/snack.

Live update requirement:
When symptoms change, the SAME card should update within 1-2 seconds:
- title
- what was noticed
- signal chips
- primary action
- more support categories
- escalation styling
- care-team prominence

Do not create duplicate cards.

Debug requirement:
For dev only, temporarily add a small internal debug line or console log showing:
- current symptom signature
- selected primary concern
- severity level
- included support categories
This can be removed later, but I need confidence that the card is actually recalculating based on the symptom controls.

Acceptance:
In Replit preview, changing from:
1. Mild nausea only
to
2. Severe nausea + low appetite
to
3. Constipation + no bowel movement
to
4. All good/stable

should visibly produce different card titles, copy, support categories, styling and care-team prominence.
