- 8 cups water

- Aim for 8 hours

- 7–8 hours

Make recommendations feel adaptable and realistic, not rigid.

=====================================
PART 3: MEDICATION-AWARE PLAN LOGIC
=====================================

This is critical.

Plan recommendations should adapt based on:

1. DOSE LEVEL
Higher dose:
- increase hydration ranges
- simplify meals
- reduce movement intensity
- increase recovery emphasis

Lower dose:
- more normal ranges
- more movement allowed
- less aggressive hydration prompts

-------------------------------------

2. RECENT TITRATION (last 14 days)
If recently increased dose:
- simplify plan
- reduce intensity
- emphasize hydration + protein
- more “light” and “rest” options

-------------------------------------

3. TIME SINCE LAST DOSE
For weekly meds:
- 0–2 days after dose → lower appetite, lower energy → lighter plan
- 3–5 days → moderate
- later in cycle → more flexibility

-------------------------------------

4. DAILY INPUTS
If:
- appetite = very low → shift to smaller meals + protein support
- side effects = rough → simplify entire plan
- energy = great → allow more movement

=====================================

=====================================

Replace generic “Stay consistent” with medication-aware tracking.

- show: “Log your dose this week”
- show status:
  - “Dose not logged yet”
  - “Dose logged ✓”
- once logged, stop prompting for the rest of the week

If medication is DAILY:
- show: “Log today’s dose”
- track daily adherence
- allow quick tap: Taken / Missed

UI should feel:
- lightweight
- not annoying
- easy to complete in 1 tap
