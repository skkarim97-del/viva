Refactor the product from a set of separate health features into a unified daily health system.

## Core Product Concept

Viva is a daily health and wellness coach that produces ONE unified output:

* the user’s daily state
* their daily focus
* a single integrated plan

Do NOT present fitness, sleep, nutrition, and mental health as separate systems.

All inputs should be combined into one clear daily recommendation.

---

## Replace “Today’s Plan” with “Your Day”

Remove siloed categories like:

* Workout
* Nutrition
* Recovery
* Sleep

Replace with:

Your Day:

* Move
* Fuel
* Recover
* Mind

Each item should:

* be short
* be actionable
* reflect the user’s overall condition

---

## Add Daily State

At the top of the screen, show one of:

* Recover
* Maintain
* Build
* Push

This should summarize the user’s current condition.

---

## Add Daily Focus

Add one short line describing the intention of the day.

Examples:

* Focus on recovery
* Stay consistent today
* Good day to build
* You can push today

---

## Integrate all systems

The system should combine:

* wearable data
* sleep data
* recovery data
* activity
* nutrition goals
* user-reported feeling
* stress level

Into one unified output.

---

## Mental health integration

Do not create a separate mental health section.

Instead, integrate mental and emotional guidance into the “Mind” section.

Examples:

* take a short walk outside
* reduce stimulation tonight
* do a short breathing exercise
* keep stress low today

---

## Sleep integration

Do not present sleep as raw data on the main screen.

Translate it into behavior:

* go to bed earlier
* keep bedtime consistent
* prioritize rest tonight

---

## Nutrition integration

Do not present complex macros.

Translate into simple guidance:

* eat balanced meals
* prioritize protein
* stay hydrated

---

## UX requirement

The Today screen must feel like:

* one cohesive story
* one clear plan
* one decision system

Not multiple separate systems.

---

## Final goal

When the user opens the app, they should feel:

“I understand my day in 5 seconds.”

Not:
“I need to interpret multiple metrics.”
